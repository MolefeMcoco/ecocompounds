Eco Compounds Furniture

We manufacture outdoor furniture that have unique resemblance to timber, as well as any custom design a customer might require.


100% Recycled Plastic

Recycled plastic with No Wood Chips or Wood Shavings added to the plastic.


Weather Resistance

These products have a longer lifespan than wood and are more resistant to weather and other natural elements.


Environmental Friendly

Our furniture do not contain harmful compounds and can be processed and reused to make new items later.

What Eco Compounds furniture offers

We offer all our clients either from our range or allow you to provide us with your own ideas, no one product is ever the same as our clients have very unique ideas. Our products being basically maintenance-free and can last as long as up to 25 years.

We manufacture from scratch so that a product can go directly to clients, our range includes picnic sets, lapa sets, school benches and monkey-proof dustbins.
